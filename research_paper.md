# Axiom-Zero: A Sovereign Neural Architecture for Algorithmic Macro-Governance

## Abstract
This project introduces **Axiom-Zero**, an autonomous neural engine designed to synthesize complex urban and economic data into predictive policy frameworks. By utilizing advanced linear regression models and heuristic data integration, Axiom-Zero bridges strategic national mandates with empirical data resilience, offering a new paradigm in autonomous state-level decision logic.

## Methodology
- **Data Integration:** The system processes multidimensional datasets, including Urban Resilience, Economic KPIs, and Infrastructure metrics.
- **Predictive Modeling:** Axiom-Zero employs regression algorithms to project future trends, allowing for proactive governance.
- **Strategic Alignment:** The engine is architected to align computational outputs with long-term national development goals.
